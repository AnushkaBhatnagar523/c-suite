Audit & Assurance Services

Enhancing Financial Credibility & Governance

Overview

Auditing is not just a statutory requirement but a tool for improving business processes and ensuring financial transparency. Our assurance services provide stakeholders with the confidence they need in your financial reporting.

Applicable Acts

  • Companies Act, 2013
  • Income Tax Act, 1961 (Tax Audit)
  • Standards on Auditing (SA) issued by ICAI

Services Offered

  • Statutory Audit of Companies
  • Tax Audit under Section 44AB
  • GST Audit & Reconciliation
  • Internal Audit & Process Review
  • Concurrent Audit for Banks
  • Management Audit & Due Diligence

Who Should Opt?

Private and Public Limited companies, large firms meeting statutory thresholds, and businesses seeking investment or bank financing must undergo regular audits to maintain regulatory standing and operational excellence.
