Labour Law Compliance

Social Security & Workplace Regulation Management

Overview

Ensuring the welfare of your workforce is not just a moral obligation but a legal one. Our Labour Law experts help you navigate the complex landscape of employee benefits and statutory contributions.

Applicable Acts

  • Employees' Provident Funds (EPF) Act, 1952
  • Employees' State Insurance (ESI) Act, 1948
  • The Factories Act, 1948
  • The Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972
  • Labour Welfare Fund (LWF) Acts

Services Offered

  • PF & ESI Registration & Monthly Filings
  • Labour Welfare Fund (LWF) Compliance
  • Payroll Processing & Statutory Deductions
  • Maintenance of Statutory Registers & Inspection Support
  • Gratuity Calculation & Management
  • Shop & Establishment Act Registration (Gumasta)

Who Should Opt?

Any establishment employing more than a specific number of employees (usually 10 or 20, depending on the Act) must comply with these social security and workplace regulations.
