Compliance & Registrations

Ensuring Regulatory Excellence & Entity Health

Overview

Maintaining a "compliant" status is critical for any entity to avoid heavy penalties and strikes from the Registrar. We handle all recurring and one-time registration requirements for your business.

Applicable Acts

  • Companies Act, 2013
  • Limited Liability Partnership Act, 2008
  • Societies Registration Act, 1860
  • Indian Trusts Act, 1882
  • Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA), 1999

Services Offered

  • Annual MCA Filings: Hassle-free filing of AOC-4, MGT-7, and Form 8/11 for LLPs.
  • Society & Trust Setup: End-to-end registration under the Societies Registration Act or Trust Act.
  • NGO Compliance: 80G/12A registration, CSR-1 filing, and NGO Darpan registration.
  • Event-Based Filings: Documentation for change in directors, trustees, or registered office.
  • Amendments & Renewals: Handling periodic license renewals and constitution amendments.

Who Should Opt?

Every registered Private Limited Company, One Person Company (OPC), and Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) in India must adhere to these compliance mandates regardless of their activity level.
