GST Services

Comprehensive Indirect Tax Management

Overview

Goods and Services Tax (GST) has transformed the indirect tax landscape in India. While it simplifies the tax structure, the compliance burden – including frequent filings and rigorous reconciliation – remains high. Our GST desk provides accurate, technology-driven solutions to manage your tax footprint across multiple states.

We assist businesses in unlocking stuck Input Tax Credit (ITC), ensuring seamless registration, and responding to technical queries from the portal, allowing you to maintain a clean compliance record.

Applicable Acts

  • Central GST (CGST) Act, 2017
  • State GST (SGST) Acts, 2017
  • Integrated GST (IGST) Act, 2017
  • GST (Compensation to States) Act, 2017

Services Offered

  • GST Registration: New registrations, amendments, and revocations for all business types.
  • Monthly Compliance: Accurate filing of GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, and CMP-08.
  • Annual Reconciliation: Comprehensive filing of GSTR-9 and 9C with detailed audit trails.
  • ITC Maximization: Regular reconciliation with GSTR-2A/2B to prevent credit leakage.
  • Department Liaison: Representation for GST audits, investigation responses, and refund claims.

Who Should Opt?

Any business with turnover exceeding prescribed limits, or those engaged in interstate supply, e-commerce, or wanting to pass on ITC to their customers, must undergo GST registration and compliance.
